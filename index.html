CytoSwitch is a Munich-based biotech project to develop smart probes and cancer chemotherapeutics.

Our technology is based on our unique, light-targeted research drugs called photostatins, recently published in the prestigious scientific journal Cell.
CytoSwitch will commercialise photostatins for research use, and in parallel will take them towards clinical trials as therapeutics that treat refractory cancer tumours without giving side-effects.
