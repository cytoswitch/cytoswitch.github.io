CytoSwitch

Targeted Chemotherapeutics & Precision Research Tools

About CytoSwitch

      We have developed a targeted antimitotic drug called Photostatin (PST), recently published in the prestigious scientific journal Cell. PST is a unique drug as it can be targeted to specific cells while avoiding all others, just by using light. Our aim is to use PST as a novel chemotherapeutic that targets cancer tumours while avoiding the healthy tissues. Due to this unique targeting mechanism, PST could therefore have much reduced side-effects compared to current chemotherapies, when used at similar dosage.

• PST could tackle refractory tumours, which do not respond to tolerable doses of current drugs.

• PST could treat weaker patients, who cannot tolerate the "normal" side-effects of current treatments.

     CytoSwitch will take PST towards clinical trials as tumour-targeted chemotherapeutics. In parallel, we will commercialise them as precision research tools to control the cytoskeleton in an unprecedented way.

CytoSwitch is currently in the pre-startup phase.
